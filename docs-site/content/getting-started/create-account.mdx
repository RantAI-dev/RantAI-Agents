# Create Account

Get started by logging into RantAI Agents for the first time.

## Login

1. Open your browser and navigate to the login page.

   <Screenshot src="/images/getting-started/login-page.png" alt="Login Page" />

2. Enter your credentials:
   - **Email** — Your email address
   - **Password** — Your password (click the eye icon to show/hide)

3. Click **Sign In** to access the dashboard.

> Demo credentials are displayed below the login form for testing purposes.

## First Look

After logging in, you'll be redirected to the dashboard.

<Screenshot src="/images/getting-started/dashboard-first-look.png" alt="Dashboard First Look" />

This is your home base — from here you can access all features of RantAI Agents.
