# Live Chat

The Live Chat portal lets human operators handle customer conversations that have been handed off from AI assistants. Conversations arrive here when a customer requests human help through any [Channel](/docs/channels) — web widget, WhatsApp, or email.

<Screenshot src="/images/live-chat/agent-portal.png" alt="Agent Portal" />

## How It Works

1. A customer starts a conversation through a [Channel](/docs/channels) (widget, WhatsApp, email)
2. The AI assistant handles the conversation automatically
3. The customer requests human help → the conversation is **handed off**
4. The conversation appears in the Live Chat queue
5. An operator goes **Online**, accepts the conversation, and responds directly

## The Agent Portal

Navigate to **Live Chat** in the dashboard sidebar to open the agent portal. The layout has two panels: the **Queue** on the left and the **Conversation Workspace** on the right.

---

### Going Online

Before you can receive conversations, you need to toggle your status to **Online**.

<Screenshot src="/images/live-chat/queue-panel-offline.png" alt="Queue Panel Offline" />

Use the **Online / Offline** switch at the top of the queue panel. When offline, the queue shows a message telling you to go online.

---

### Queue Panel

Once you're online, the queue shows customers waiting for help.

<Screenshot src="/images/live-chat/queue-panel-online.png" alt="Queue Panel Online" />

Each entry displays:
- **Customer name** (or "Anonymous" if not provided)
- **Channel source** — Badge showing where the request came from (PORTAL, WHATSAPP, etc.)
- **Email** — Customer's email address
- **Product interest** — What the customer is interested in
- **Wait time** — How long the customer has been waiting
- **Last message** — Preview of their most recent message (truncated)
- **Accept** button — Click to accept and start helping

---

### Accepting a Conversation

Click **Accept** on a customer in the queue. The conversation moves from the queue to the **Active** section at the top of the panel.

<Screenshot src="/images/live-chat/queue-accept.png" alt="Queue After Accept" />

The active conversation shows the customer name, email, and product interest. Click it to bring the conversation back into focus.

---

### Conversation Workspace

Once you accept a conversation, the workspace opens on the right.

#### Header

- Customer name, email, and product interest badge
- **Resolve** button — Mark the conversation as done

#### Message Thread

<Screenshot src="/images/live-chat/conversation-messages.png" alt="Conversation Messages" />

Full conversation history including previous AI assistant messages. Messages are color-coded by sender:

| Color | Sender |
|---|---|
| **Blue** | Customer messages |
| **Green** | Your (agent) messages |
| **Violet** | AI assistant messages |
| **Gray (centered)** | System messages |

Hover over any message to **copy** it to clipboard.

#### Typing Indicator

When the customer is typing, animated dots appear to let you know a message is coming.

#### Message Input

- Text area at the bottom
- **Enter** to send, **Shift+Enter** for new line
- Send button on the right

---

### Resolving a Conversation

Click the **Resolve** button in the header to mark the conversation as complete. The conversation is closed, and the workspace returns to the empty state.

---

## Empty States

The workspace shows different messages depending on your status:

### Offline

"You're Offline" — Toggle your status to online to start receiving requests.

### Online, Customers Waiting

"X Customer(s) Waiting" — Select a customer from the queue to start helping them.

### Online, Queue Empty

"Ready to Help" — You're online and ready. New customer requests will appear in the queue.

---

## Enabling Live Chat

<Screenshot src="/images/live-chat/enable-live-chat.png" alt="Enable Live Chat" />

To enable Live Chat:

1. Go to **Settings → General**
2. Enable the **AGENT** feature flag
3. In the **Agent Builder**, open your assistant's **Configure** tab
4. Toggle **Enable Live Chat** handoff

## Conversation Sources

The queue shows which channel each conversation came from:

| Channel | Source Label |
|---|---|
| Embed Widget | PORTAL |
| WhatsApp | WHATSAPP |
| Email | EMAIL |

## Tips

- Toggle **Online** to start receiving conversations — you won't see anything while offline
- Review the AI assistant's previous responses (violet messages) before replying
- Click **Resolve** when done to clear the conversation and free up for the next customer
- Your online status is saved — if you refresh the page, you'll automatically rejoin
