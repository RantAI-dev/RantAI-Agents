# Groups

> This feature is part of **Digital Employees** and is currently under development.

Groups let you organize digital employees into teams with shared resources and bulk operations.

## Planned Capabilities

- **Create teams** — Group digital employees by function or department
- **Shared configuration** — Apply common settings across a team
- **Bulk operations** — Manage multiple employees at once
- **Member management** — Assign and remove employees from groups
