# Workflows

Browse pre-built automation pipelines that you can use as templates.

<Screenshot src="/images/marketplace/marketplace-workflows.png" alt="Workflows Tab" />

## What Are Marketplace Workflows?

These are pre-built workflow templates with pre-configured nodes, connections, triggers, and variables. Use them as a starting point and customize to fit your needs.

## How to Use

1. Navigate to **Marketplace** → **Workflows** tab
2. Browse or search for a workflow
3. Click **Use Template** on the one you want
4. You'll be taken to the **Workflow Editor** with the template pre-loaded
5. Customize the nodes, connections, and settings as needed
6. Save your workflow

> Workflows use the **"Use Template"** action — they create a new workflow in your editor based on the template, rather than installing a shared component.

## After Using a Template

The new workflow appears in your **Workflows** list as a draft. You can:

- Modify nodes and connections on the canvas
- Update trigger settings (manual, webhook, schedule)
- Change variables and parameters
- Set it to **Active** when ready
