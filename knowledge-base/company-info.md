# HorizonLife Insurance Company Information

## About HorizonLife

### Our Story

Founded in 2010, HorizonLife Insurance has grown from a small regional provider to one of America's most trusted insurance companies. What started as a mission to make quality insurance accessible has evolved into a comprehensive suite of protection products serving over 500,000 customers nationwide.
