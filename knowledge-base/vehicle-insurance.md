## Vehicle Insurance Claims

### What to Do After an Accident

1. **Ensure Safety** — Move to a safe location if possible. Call 911 if there are injuries.
2. **Exchange Information** — Get other driver's name, insurance, license plate, phone number.
3. **Document the Scene** — Take photos of damage, road conditions, traffic signs, and positions of vehicles.
4. **File a Police Report** — Required for all accidents involving injuries or significant damage.
5. **Contact HorizonLife** — Call 1-800-HORIZON or use the mobile app to report within 24 hours.
6. **Do NOT Admit Fault** — Let the insurance companies determine liability.

### Required Documents for Vehicle Claims

| Document | Required | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Completed Claim Form | Yes | Form VI-400 |
| Police Report | Yes (if applicable) | Report number at minimum |
| Photos of Damage | Yes | All angles, close-up and wide shots |
| Other Driver's Information | Yes | Insurance card, license, contact info |
| Witness Information | If available | Names and phone numbers |
| Repair Estimates | Recommended | At least 2 estimates from approved shops |
| Medical Bills | If injury | For medical payment claims |
| Rental Car Receipts | If applicable | For rental reimbursement claims |

## Eligibility Requirements

- **Age**: Primary driver must be 16+ (under 25 may have surcharge)
- **License**: Valid driver's license required
- **Vehicle**: Must be registered and pass state safety inspection
- **Driving Record**: Reviewed for last 5 years. DUI/DWI within 3 years may result in decline or surcharge.
- **Vehicle Use**: Personal, commute, or business. Commercial vehicles require separate commercial auto policy.

### Vehicles We Cover

- Cars and SUVs (model year 1990 and newer)
- Trucks (up to 1 ton)
- Motorcycles (separate motorcycle policy)
- RVs and motorhomes
- Classic/antique vehicles (25+ years old, agreed value policy)

### Vehicles We Do NOT Cover

- Commercial fleet vehicles (10+ units)
- Semi-trucks and tractor-trailers
- All-terrain vehicles (ATVs) for off-road use only
- Golf carts (unless street-legal and registered)
- Vehicles used for racing
