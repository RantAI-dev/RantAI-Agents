# HorizonLife Claim Procedures

## Overview

Filing a claim with HorizonLife is designed to be straightforward and transparent. This guide covers the step-by-step process for each type of insurance claim, required documentation, estimated timelines, and what to expect at each stage.

---

## General Claim Process

All HorizonLife claims follow this general flow:

1. **Report the Incident** — Notify HorizonLife within 30 days of the event
2. **Submit Documentation** — Provide required forms and supporting documents
3. **Claim Review** — Our team reviews and verifies the claim
4. **Assessment** — Adjuster evaluates the claim (if applicable)
5. **Decision** — Claim approved, partially approved, or denied with explanation
6. **Payment** — Approved claims paid within 5-10 business days

### How to File a Claim

- **Online**: Log in to your account at horizonlife.com/claims
- **Phone**: Call 1-800-HORIZON (1-800-467-4966), press 2 for claims
- **Email**: Send to claims@horizonlife.com
- **In-Person**: Visit any HorizonLife branch office
- **Mobile App**: Use the HorizonLife app to file and track claims

## Life Insurance Claims

### Required Documents

| Document | Required | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Completed Claim Form | Yes | Form LI-100, available online or at branches |
| Certified Death Certificate | Yes | Original or certified copy |
| Policy Document | Yes | Original policy or policy number |
| Beneficiary ID | Yes | Government-issued photo ID of each beneficiary |
| Proof of Relationship | If applicable | Marriage certificate, birth certificate, etc. |
| Medical Records | If requested | Last attending physician statement |
| Police Report | If applicable | For accidental death claims |
| Autopsy Report | If applicable | If cause of death is under investigation |

### Process Timeline

| Stage | Estimated Time |
|-------|---------------|
| Initial Review | 3-5 business days |
| Document Verification | 5-10 business days |
| Investigation (if needed) | 15-30 business days |
| Payment Processing | 5-10 business days after approval |
| **Total (standard)** | **15-25 business days** |
| **Total (with investigation)** | **30-55 business days** |

### Step-by-Step

1. **Notify HorizonLife** — Contact us as soon as possible after the policyholder's passing. Have the policy number ready.
2. **Receive Claim Kit** — We'll send a claim form package within 2 business days, or you can download it immediately online.
3. **Gather Documents** — Collect all required documents listed above.
4. **Submit Claim** — Send completed forms and documents via mail, email, or upload online.
5. **Claim Acknowledgment** — You'll receive confirmation within 3 business days.
6. **Review & Verification** — Our team verifies all documents and policy terms.
7. **Decision Notification** — Written notification of approval or denial.
8. **Payment** — Beneficiaries choose payment method: lump sum check, direct deposit, or annuity option.

### Special Circumstances

- **Accidental Death**: Additional Accidental Death Benefit (ADB) requires police report and accident investigation report.
- **Death Within First 2 Years**: Subject to contestability period review. Additional documentation may be requested.
- **Multiple Beneficiaries**: Each beneficiary must submit their own ID and claim form. Payment is split per policy designation.

## Health Insurance Claims

### Required Documents

| Document | Required | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Completed Claim Form | Yes | Form HI-200 |
| Itemized Medical Bills | Yes | With procedure codes (CPT/ICD-10) |
| Explanation of Benefits (EOB) | If applicable | From primary insurer if secondary |
| Referral Authorization | If applicable | For specialist visits |
| Pre-Authorization | If applicable | For planned surgeries/procedures |
| Prescription Records | If applicable | For pharmacy claims |
| Medical Records | If requested | Treatment notes from provider |

### Process Timeline

| Stage | Estimated Time |
|-------|---------------|
| Initial Review | 2-3 business days |
| Provider Verification | 3-5 business days |
| Adjudication | 5-10 business days |
| Payment/Reimbursement | 5-7 business days after approval |
| **Total (in-network)** | **10-15 business days** |
| **Total (out-of-network)** | **15-25 business days** |

### Step-by-Step

1. **Receive Treatment** — Visit an in-network provider whenever possible for faster processing.
2. **In-Network Claims**: Your provider files the claim directly — no action needed from you.
3. **Out-of-Network Claims**: Pay the provider, then submit Form HI-200 with itemized bills.
4. **Submit Within 90 Days** — All health claims must be filed within 90 days of service.
5. **Claim Processing** — We verify coverage, apply deductibles, co-pays, and co-insurance.
6. **EOB Issued** — You receive an Explanation of Benefits showing what was covered.
7. **Payment** — In-network: we pay the provider. Out-of-network: reimbursement sent to you.

### Common Health Claim Scenarios

- **Emergency Room Visit**: Always covered regardless of network. Submit ER records within 90 days.
- **Prescription Drugs**: Use your HorizonLife pharmacy card at participating pharmacies. For mail-order, use horizonlife.com/pharmacy.
- **Mental Health**: Covered same as physical health. No separate pre-authorization needed.
- **Preventive Care**: Annual checkups, vaccinations, and screenings at 100% coverage with no deductible.

## Home Insurance Claims

### Required Documents

| Document | Required | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Completed Claim Form | Yes | Form HO-300 |
| Photos/Video of Damage | Yes | Before and after cleanup/repairs |
| Police Report | If applicable | For theft, vandalism, or break-in |
| Contractor Estimates | Recommended | At least 2 repair estimates |
| Inventory of Damaged Items | Yes | List with approximate values |
| Receipts/Proof of Purchase | If available | For personal property claims |
| Mortgage Company Info | If applicable | Checks over $10,000 may include mortgagee |

### Process Timeline

| Stage | Estimated Time |
|-------|---------------|
| Initial Report | Immediate |
| Adjuster Assignment | 1-3 business days |
| Property Inspection | 3-7 business days |
| Estimate Preparation | 5-10 business days |
| Claim Decision | 2-5 business days after inspection |
| Payment | 5-10 business days after approval |
| **Total (minor claim)** | **10-20 business days** |
| **Total (major claim)** | **20-45 business days** |

### Step-by-Step

1. **Secure the Property** — Prevent further damage (e.g., tarp a leaking roof, board up broken windows). Keep receipts for emergency repairs.
2. **Document Everything** — Take photos and videos of all damage before cleanup.
3. **Report the Claim** — Call 1-800-HORIZON or file online within 48 hours of discovery.
4. **Claim Number Assigned** — You'll receive a claim number and adjuster contact info.
5. **Adjuster Visit** — A claims adjuster will inspect the damage in person.
6. **Estimate & Decision** — We prepare a repair estimate and coverage determination.
7. **Payment** — Initial payment minus deductible. Supplemental payments if additional damage is found.

### Important Notes

- **Emergency Repairs**: You can make temporary emergency repairs to prevent further damage. HorizonLife will reimburse reasonable costs.
- **Deductible**: Your deductible applies to each claim. Standard deductibles: $500, $1,000, or $2,500.
- **Replacement Cost vs. Actual Cash Value**: If you have Replacement Cost coverage, you'll receive the full replacement amount. Actual Cash Value deducts depreciation.
- **Living Expenses**: If your home is uninhabitable, Additional Living Expense (ALE) coverage pays for temporary housing, meals, and other costs up to your policy limit.

## Claim Status Tracking

### How to Check Your Claim Status

1. **Online Portal**: Log in at horizonlife.com/claims/status
2. **Mobile App**: Open the HorizonLife app → Claims → Track Status
3. **Phone**: Call 1-800-HORIZON, press 2, then enter your claim number
4. **Email**: Send your claim number to claimstatus@horizonlife.com

### Claim Status Stages

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Received** | Claim has been logged in our system |
| **Under Review** | Documents are being verified |
| **Adjuster Assigned** | An adjuster has been assigned to your claim |
| **Investigation** | Additional information is being gathered |
| **Assessment Complete** | Evaluation is done, decision pending |
| **Approved** | Claim approved, payment being processed |
| **Partially Approved** | Some items covered, explanation provided |
| **Denied** | Claim not covered, denial letter with reasons sent |
| **Payment Sent** | Payment has been issued |
| **Closed** | Claim fully resolved |

## Appeals and Escalation

### If Your Claim is Denied

1. **Review the Denial Letter** — Understand the specific reason for denial.
2. **Gather Additional Evidence** — Collect any supporting documents that address the denial reason.
3. **File an Appeal** — Submit Form AP-400 within 60 days of denial.
4. **Appeal Review** — A senior claims specialist reviews within 15 business days.
5. **Final Decision** — Written response with detailed explanation.

### Escalation Path

1. **Claims Representative** — Initial point of contact
2. **Claims Supervisor** — If unsatisfied with representative's response
3. **Claims Manager** — For complex or disputed claims
4. **Ombudsman** — Independent review for unresolved disputes
5. **State Insurance Commissioner** — External regulatory complaint (last resort)

### Contact for Escalation

- **Claims Supervisor**: 1-800-HORIZON ext. 3001
- **Claims Manager**: claimsmanager@horizonlife.com
- **Ombudsman Office**: ombudsman@horizonlife.com or 1-800-HORIZON ext. 5000

## Fraud Warning

Insurance fraud is a serious offense. HorizonLife has a dedicated Special Investigations Unit (SIU) that reviews suspicious claims. Fraudulent claims may result in:

- Claim denial
- Policy cancellation
- Criminal prosecution
- Civil penalties

If you suspect insurance fraud, report it anonymously at 1-800-HORIZON ext. 9999 or fraud@horizonlife.com.
